About Asir Justifus

Asir Justifus is an entrepreneur with value-based marketplace mindset, ecosystem builder, and author, whose ventures span logistics, talent tech, housing innovation, integration and digital transformation. He is the Founder and Chairman of SART Accelerator, where he mentors startups and scales ventures across the USA, India, and the Middle East.

Born in July, 1971, in Waterfall Estate, Valparai - a serene tea plantation nestled in the Anaimalai hills of Pollachi, Tamil Nadu - Asir's early life was shaped by nature, tradition, and community. He grew up in Santhapuram, a small village near Nagercoil in the Kanyakumari district, where the values of faith, dignity, and perseverance were deeply woven into his upbringing.

Over the years, Asir’s journey as an entrepreneur has enriched his expertise across industries. Fueled by a passion for innovation and a relentless drive for growth, Asir Justifus continues to lead as a visionary entrepreneur. His journey is defined by the transformation of bold ideas into meaningful solutions - solutions that deliver measurable value and help shape the future of the startups and ventures he champions. Asir Justifus, A Visionary Entrepreneur Driving Transformation Across Industries

From the tea plantation companies of Tamil Nadu to boardrooms across continents, Asir Justifus has carved a legacy as a strategic problem solver, solution building as an entrepreneur, ecosystem builder, and global logistics innovator. His leadership journey is a masterclass in seeing solutions for problems and challenges at least 10 years ahead, putting together the blueprint needed to transform the visionary ideas into scalable, purpose-driven ventures.

Founder of Game-Changing Platforms

Asir launched Tranzlogix in 2012, which evolved into TruckerCloud in 2020 after securing investment from Rule1 Ventures, Buckhead, Atlanta, revolutionizing logistics visibility and operations for Risk Management by leveraging Electronic Logging Devices (ELD) data through Telematics. He didn't stop there - he built ELDMarketplace.com, TruckerGIG, and EnterKey Solutions, each designed to streamline supply chains and empower digital transformation.

Global Influence in Logistics

Asir founded Trusted Roadways and Logistics Pvt Ltd in 2004, which later evolved into Tranzlogix Solutions. He also founded United Technology Solutions (India) Pvt Ltd. in 2004 and later merged with Compvue in 2018. His international footprint includes a Board Member role at the Logistics & Transportation Association of North America (LTNA) - shaping the future of global mobility and innovation.

Champion of Talent & Tech

With TalentTurbo launched in 2016, Asir created a platform that not only connects job seekers to opportunities but also monetizes talent discovery. His work at SART Accelerator fuels startup growth through strategic marketing, sales acceleration, and founder mentorship.

Beyond Logistics: Expanding Horizons

Every venture he touches reflects a relentless drive to build smarter systems, empower people, and create lasting impact. Whether he's launching startups, advising founders, or steering global conversations, Asir Justifus leads with vision, velocity, and values.

SART Accelerator has diversified into Eco Tourism. For this, Ecosystems such as RomarKing, SimpleGo Taxi and HomieFix platforms have evolved. RomarKing is into FRP (Fiber Reinforced Polymer) based prefabricated housing and the ecosystem, bringing sustainable, disaster-resilient architecture to mountainous terrains towards conserving nature.

Through SART Accelerator, Asir is also associated with the FinTech Digital Negotiation and Settlement platform SeTLD and also Digital Social Scoring platform tiipstr. SART Accelerator has invested in Automated Deal Resolution Exchange (ADRX), USA based group that has developed proprietary Smart Settle Technology and Smart Score Technology. The 1971 Imprint

A Life of Purpose, Connection & Reinvention

Born into a World of Change

The year 1971 marked a turning point in global history - a time when nations were redefining themselves, technology was shifting from mainframes to microchips, and a new generation of leaders, artists, and innovators was quietly emerging.

Among them was Asir Justifus, born in Santhapuram, Nagercoil, in the southern tip of Tamil Nadu, India. His early life was steeped in the rhythms of family, faith, and community, where values like dignity, perseverance, and service were not just taught but lived. These foundational principles would later become the compass for his entrepreneurial journey.

A Generational Kinship

Asir shares his birth year (1971) with a remarkable cohort of changemakers:

  • Volodymyr Zelenskyy – Leading with courage
  • Justin Trudeau – Governing a diverse democracy
  • Mary J. Blige, Snoop Dogg, Ricky Martin – Reshaping music
  • Winona Ryder, Ewan McGregor, Sandra Oh – Defining global cinema
  • Pete Sampras, Lance Armstrong – Testing human endurance

Though their paths diverged, they all carried the 1971 imprint - a calling to challenge, disrupt, and rebuild.

The First Leap: Late 1990s

Asir's first overseas engagement in the late 1990s was a defining moment. It exposed him to the power of global networks and the promise of entrepreneurship. What began as a fascination with transportation and systems evolved into a mission to build ventures that solve real-world problems.

Armed with little more than faith, grit, and vision, he stepped into the world of startups - transforming his passion into platforms that empower others.

Philosophy & Vision

A Philosophy Rooted in Values

Over the decades, Asir's journey has been marked by:

  • Accomplishments and setbacks
  • Alliances and separations
  • Obligations and dreams

Yet through it all, he held fast to a core belief:

"Progress without people is shallow. True growth must honor human networks, value systems, and the dignity of life itself."

This philosophy is reflected in his ventures, mentorship, and writing - especially in his book Internet of Humans, which envisions a world where technology serves connection, not isolation.

The Internet of Humans

The Internet of Humans

Asir's generational calling culminates in his vision for the Internet of Humans - a network not just of machines, but of people bound by trust, collaboration, and purpose. It's a response to the fragmentation of modern life and a blueprint for restoring meaningful relationships in business, society, and beyond.
